WHAT IS SAITI?

Gas and Oil Industry

R.-I.S Oil & Gas with his partener SAITI Limited is an Angolan company which started its activities in 2008. The company are registered in the Petroleum Ministry Under No. 97338 / 2018. So, far the Company has 73 employees working in diferent sector in Angola. Presently, our company has construction contrats with ENI and PLUSPETROL including Man Power to FRIELANDER for Maintenance activities.

Our Personnel are trained and qualified in accordance with international standards with appropriate experience and technology with strong organizations capabilities to meet customer requirement.

The main office is actually located in Luanda Rua dos Coqueiros , Lote 37, Bairro Ingombota (Muntamba). In 2017 new office has been created at Cabinda province with the installation of new yard for supporting PLUSPETROL and ENI activities.

Our company has a partnership with DEVONIA a south African company to assist us in engeering.
